Our Beginning

From Degloor to the World

Vyankyaa Foods was born in Degloor, Dist. Nanded — a region where agriculture is not just livelihood, it's identity. Founder Baalaje V. Kadaam started with a simple but powerful belief: India's farmers grow some of the world's finest produce, and the world deserves to taste it.

What began as a local trading venture evolved into a fully-fledged export enterprise, built on trust, transparency, and an unwavering commitment to quality. Today, Vyankyaa Foods ships to over 50 countries across the Middle East, Europe, Africa, and Southeast Asia.

Every export represents a partnership — between us, our farmers, and our global buyers. We don't just move goods; we move the story of Indian agriculture forward.

The Founder

Baalaje V. Kadaam

An export mentor and global trade coach, Baalaje V. Kadaam has dedicated his career to connecting Indian agriculture with international markets. With deep roots in Maharashtra's farming communities and a sharp understanding of global trade dynamics, he built Vyankyaa Foods into a trusted name among importers and distributors worldwide.

A passionate advocate for farmers, Baalaje works directly with over 200 agricultural partners to ensure fair pricing, sustainable practices, and consistently premium output — season after season.
